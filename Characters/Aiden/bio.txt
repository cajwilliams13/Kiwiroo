Aiden spent his life wandering through the Realm, learning all he could from his travels. He met all sorts of people and creatures, in all sorts of places. From running away from pirates to defending a city against bandits, Aiden had plenty of stories. On one of his adventures, he met Valera, leader of the Resistance, who inspired him to join their cause. As long as he could see more of the world, he had absolutely no complaints.
