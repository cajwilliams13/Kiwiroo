Orphan at birth with her older twin sister Scarlet with the only things they had were a necklace for amber and a spell book for scarlet Amber and her sister would live with a group of ska'drin for most of their childhood before leaving to go out and find their people. Once the war for crystal magic started Amber learned her necklace wasn't some piece of fashion but a power relic of her people back from the great scourge as it had a sealed fire elemental locked inside that gifted the wearing increased power to the matching elemental magic type, threw battles faught with the resistance she would learn the sacred arts her people used to help end the scourge long ago
